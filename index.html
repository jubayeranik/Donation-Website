Our Donate Plan

Our advocacy efforts focus on policy reform to remove systemic barriers preventing low-income and historically underserved families from accessing adequate, affordable shelter.

Food for a family

There are many family can't eat properly. Our goal is to give them food so that they can eat. Providing lunch helps our students engage and focus on a much-needed education to give a chance to access employment and break the poverty cycle.

Shelter for a family

We are there when a family’s homelessness is imminent, and we work to avoid the trauma of a family losing their housing. When families have lost housing and reach out to Family Promise, we provide alternatives to shelter

Education for a child

Students and youngsters are Anik Foundation champions in the struggle to overcome the scourge of substandard housing. Anik Foundation engages school and college students along with youth groups to join other young people around the world who are serving with those in need.

About Us

Anik Foundation is a nonprofit that supports other nonprofits by connecting them to donors and companies. Since 2002, we've helped trusted, community-led organizations from Afghanistan to Zimbabwe (and hundreds of places in between) access the tools, training, and support they need to make our world a better place.
